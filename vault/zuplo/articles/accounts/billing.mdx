The "Upgrade & Billing" page in the Zuplo dashboard allows you to subscribe to a Zuplo plan, update your payment method, and view your billing history.

Zuplo uses Stripe to process payments. When you subscribe to a Zuplo plan, you will be redirected to the Stripe checkout page to enter your payment information.

## Update Payment Method

When you navigate to the "Upgrade & Billing" page, you will see a link to manage your existing subscription.

:::note{title="Enterprise Plans"}

Enterprise plans are setup with custom billing and can't be managed through the portal.

:::
