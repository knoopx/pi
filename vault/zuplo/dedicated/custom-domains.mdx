Configuration of custom domains with your managed dedicated instance of Zuplo varies depending on how your [instance networking is configured](./networking.mdx).

Custom configurations are supported with Zuplo Managed Dedicated instances.

For the full details on how to configure custom domains with your managed dedicated instance of Zuplo, please contact your account manager.

## Preview environments

By default, preview environments are deployed to a wildcard subdomain managed by Zuplo.

Some customer want to use their own domain for preview environments. For example, `*.dev.acme.com`.
